About Us

Shri. P.Shunmugam laid the foundation for P.S.Tamarind in Kumbakonam, a town located in South India, in 1938. As a diligent entrepreneur, he implemented innovative methodologies to attract and retain loyal customers, while thoughtfully expanding his markets.

In 1950, he pioneered innovative packaging techniques, presenting Tamarind in a distinctive cuboid form under the brand 'P.S Special Packet Tamarind'. This product swiftly became a household name, synonymous with quality.

Equipped with exceptional business skills and distinctive marketing strategies, he swiftly gained the trust of the Indian populace and ventured into international markets.

In 1968, P.S Tamarind marked its debut in the European markets, a significant milestone under the visionary leadership of Mr. P. Shunmugam. Guided by his stewardship, P.S.Tamarind underwent remarkable expansion, evolving into a sought-after product. Now distributed across various regions, spanning the West, Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Europe.
